Super Master Franchise

What is a Super Master Franchise?

The Super Master Franchise (SMF) operates as a franchisee-owned, independent business at the district level. It offers a 3% ROI on the total business generated within the defined territory. This role is ideal for those looking to manage a large-scale operation and build a substantial network of resellers.

Benefits

  • Exclusive territory rights at the district level.
  • Authority to sell, install, and manage products in the region.
  • Opportunity to build and manage a vast reseller network.
  • 3% ROI on total business in the defined territory.

Master Reseller

What is a Master Reseller?

Master Resellers manage distribution at the city or region level. They are responsible for overseeing operations, supporting sub-resellers, and ensuring smooth sales processes within their designated area.

Benefits

  • Manage distribution for a city or region.
  • Multiple revenue streams from direct sales and sub-reseller licenses.
  • Rapid business growth through the sale of reseller rights.
  • Recurring revenue from subscription-based digital products.
Apply Now

Sub-Reseller

What is a Sub-Reseller?

Sub-Resellers focus on local sales and service within a specific area. They work under Master Resellers and are responsible for generating sales and providing customer support at the local level.

Benefits

  • Focus on local sales and service.
  • Access to a global market.
  • Comprehensive support, including training and marketing materials.
  • Advanced tools for business management.
Apply Now

Reseller

What is a Reseller?

Resellers are the entry-level tier, focusing on direct sales to customers. They are the primary point of contact for customers and play a crucial role in driving sales and customer satisfaction.

Benefits

  • Entry-level opportunity with a focus on direct sales.
  • Exclusive reselling rights and bulk discounts.
  • White-labeling opportunities.
  • Dedicated customer support system.
